Pricing

There are 3 sections, based on your age. Additionally, there is an option for water-landing and land-landing.

Type Age Cost
Standard Infants (Under 3) Free
Standard Ages 3-12 $59.99
Standard Ages 12-65 $89.99
Standard Ages 65+ $59.99
Deluxe All Ages $99.99

Please note that it is advised that infants are prepared with a strap, and seniors are able to withstand high air pressure. It is unadvised for seniors above 90 years old to attend, as well as infants under the age of 1. SkyDev is not responsible for any injuries that meet that criteria.
